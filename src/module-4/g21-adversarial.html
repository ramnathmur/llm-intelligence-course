Adversarial Robustness

LLMs can be manipulated through adversarial prompts that bypass safety training — understanding attacks is essential for building robust agents.

~10 min
Anchor Analogy
Social engineering attacks on a company — the security system works until someone tricks the employee into bypassing it.
Why This Matters
Every user input to your agent is a potential adversarial prompt. Understanding the attack surface is essential.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can design agents that handle untrusted input safely — because you understand how safety training gets bypassed.
