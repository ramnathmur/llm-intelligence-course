The Alignment Problem

An intelligent system that cannot be directed toward human-intended goals is not useful intelligence — alignment ensures capability serves intent.

~12 min
Anchor Analogy
A brilliant but literal-minded intern: they optimize exactly what you told them, even if what you told them is not what you meant.
Why This Matters
An unaligned autonomous agent is the canonical AI safety failure mode. This transforms how you design system prompts.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you understand the core safety challenge: "Intelligence without alignment optimizes the wrong thing — and the smarter it is, the worse that gets."
