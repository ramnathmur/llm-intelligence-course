RLHF Deep Dive

RLHF trains a reward model on human preferences, then uses it to fine-tune the LLM — translating human judgment into mathematical optimization.

~12 min
Anchor Analogy
Training a new employee by showing pairs of draft emails and asking "which one would you send?" — they learn your judgment without a rulebook.
Why This Matters
The mechanics of how "be helpful, be honest, be harmless" gets translated from human judgment into model behavior.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can explain how human values get into the machine — not through rules, but through preference learning.
Prerequisites: G15 — Fine-Tuning & Transfer Learning G13 — Backpropagation & Gradient Descent
