Transformer Attention

Self-attention is a learned mechanism for routing information between tokens based on relevance — structured relational reasoning, not autocomplete.

~15 min
Anchor Analogy
A Supreme Court panel where each justice highlights different relationships in the same document, then combines verdicts.
Why This Matters
If attention = structured relational reasoning, the model is not a statistical autocomplete machine.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can dismantle "autocomplete": "The model doesn't just look at the last few words — it tracks relationships across the entire context, selectively."
