Multimodal Intelligence

Modern LLMs process images, audio, and text in a unified representation space — genuine cross-modal reasoning, not just captioning.

~10 min
Anchor Analogy
A doctor who reads the X-ray, listens to the patient, and checks lab results — intelligence that reasons across different types of information.
Why This Matters
Multimodal reasoning demonstrates that "next-token prediction" generalizes beyond text into a qualitatively richer form of intelligence.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can point out that the model reasons across images and text simultaneously — no "next-word guesser" does that.
