Advanced Reasoning

Beyond linear CoT: tree-of-thought (search), reflection (metacognition), and self-correction (error repair) — three distinct reasoning strategies.

~12 min
Anchor Analogy
A chess grandmaster who considers multiple lines of play, evaluates positions, and backtracks from bad lines.
Why This Matters
Understanding these architectures lets you design agents that explore, evaluate, and backtrack — not just think linearly.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can describe three distinct ways LLMs reason beyond simple linear thinking — search, self-evaluation, and error repair.
Prerequisites: G9 — Chain-of-Thought as Computation G6 — Compositional Generalization
