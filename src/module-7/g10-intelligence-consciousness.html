Intelligence vs. Consciousness

Intelligence (goal-directed problem-solving) and consciousness (subjective experience) are separate — LLMs clearly have the former, almost certainly lack the latter.

~12 min
Anchor Analogy
A chess engine beating a world champion: clearly intelligent, clearly not conscious. Separable properties.
Why This Matters
Settles the core anxiety: yes, it's measurably intelligent, but that does not imply sentience.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can resolve the deepest confusion: "Intelligence and consciousness are different things. The chess engine is intelligent. That doesn't make it sentient."
