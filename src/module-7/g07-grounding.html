The Grounding Question

Does an LLM understand meaning or manipulate symbols without understanding? The honest answer is somewhere in between, with evidence on both sides.

~15 min
Anchor Analogy
The Chinese Room — symbol manipulation at the component level vs. possible understanding at the system level.
Why This Matters
Distinguishing "stochastic parrot" from "genuine understanding." The truth is a defensible middle ground.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can hold the sophisticated middle position: "It's not a parrot and it's not human understanding — it's something new, with measurable evidence on both sides."
