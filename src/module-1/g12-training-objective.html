The Training Objective

Predicting the next token forces the model to learn grammar, facts, reasoning patterns, world knowledge, and theory of mind.

~8 min
Anchor Analogy
Learning cricket by predicting the next ball — to predict well, you must learn physics, player psychology, pitch conditions, and game strategy.
Why This Matters
The direct rebuttal to "it just guesses the next word." To guess well, it must learn everything humans encode in language.
After This Page, You Can Argue
After this page, you can turn the skeptic's own argument: "Yes, it predicts the next word — and to do that well, it had to learn everything."
